The Most Average Pizza You'll Ever Have

We didn't start a pizza place so you could tell us how good or bad our food is. If you don't like it then go somewhere else. If you tell us we are rude. We don't care. If you have a complaint. We don't care.

At some point you will make up your mind like everyone else whether you will come back. Maybe you like us? Maybe you like something we make? Well, good. We'll be happy to make some pizza or food for you.

Here are some things to think about before you call us:

  • We deliver, but we are not the quickest.
  • We will close when we say we close. We open when we open.
  • We have pizza and we make pasta.
  • We don't make cold subs so don't ask. Read the menu please.
  • We don't have texting, web orders, or app thingys. Call us or stop in.
  • PS Do you want to eat or not? It is called food and we have it.
